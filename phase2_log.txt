Phase 2: Project Architecture & Workflow Summary

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the SkySure (GigGuard) parametric insurance platform, detailing the technical stack, workflow, logics, and interface contents.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Project Identity & Purpose
SkySure is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed for gig workers (delivery partners). It eliminates the traditional claims process by using real-time environmental telemetry to trigger instant payouts during severe weather or urban disruptions.

3. The Dataset
The platform utilizes a structured dataset of riders and historical performance data:
- Rider Profiles: IDs, names, cities (Chennai, Coimbatore, Salem, Madurai, Trichy), and personas.
- Personas: Pro (Full-Timer), Standard (Gig-Pro), Basic (Student-Flex), Veteran (Risk Averse).
- Live Telemetry: Real-time GPS coords, session times, earning efficiency, and past_week_earnings.
- Risk Metrics: Ring scores (geospatial clustering) and trust indices.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. Trigger & Fraud Logic

Disruption Trigger Logic (7 Signals)
Disruptions are detected when a weighted sum of 7 behavioral and environmental signals exceeds the threshold:
1. Heavy Rain: > 10mm/hr (Primary Trigger)
2. Order Drop: > 30% reduction from baseline
3. Rider Inactivity: Ghosting or status changes
4. Abnormal Delivery Time: > 45 mins per order
5. Low Order Volume: < 50% of historical average
6. High Wind: > 40 km/h
7. Low Visibility: < 5 km

Adversarial Fraud Logic (6 Checks)
Before any payout is approved, it passes through an AI Fraud Engine:
1. Rain Correlation (25%): Does inactivity only happen during rain?
2. Velocity Spike (20%): Sudden unusual spike in claims?
3. Ghost Rider (20%): No GPS movement despite "trapped" status?
4. Synced Inactivity (15%): Massive cluster-wide inactivity indicating a "Ring".
5. Behavioral Anomaly (10%): Delivery patterns diverging from historical baseline.
6. Cluster Anomaly (10%): Geographic concentration of claims.

Outcome: If Fraud Score >= 30%, the payout is Mitigated (Blocked).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. Premium & Payout Structure

Adaptive Premium Calculation
- Base Rate: 0.5% - 1.5% of weekly earnings depending on the tier.
- Weather Multiplier: 1.0x to 1.5x based on the severity of the upcoming weather forecast.
- Formula: Premium = (Weekly Earnings * Base Rate) * Weather Factor

Payout Tiers (Severity-Based)
| Tier       | Score | Base Payout |
|------------|-------|-------------|
| Critical   | 5.0+  | INR 1,000   |
| High       | 3.5+  | INR 700     |
| Medium     | 2.5+  | INR 500     |
| Low        | 1.0+  | INR 300     |

Multipliers apply based on Rider Plan: Pro (1.3x) | Standard (1.0x) | Basic (0.7x).

6. Dual Interface Context

A. Admin/Enterprise Dashboard
1. Overview (Operations Hub):
   - Live KPI tracking (Total Risk Pool, Active Riders, High-Risk Actors).
   - "Network Behavior Correlation" chart using Recharts.
   - Live "Neural Intervention Feed" showing real-time approvals and mitigations.
2. Riders (Partner Database):
   - Full registry of 14K+ riders.
   - Search/Filter by city, tier, and risk level.
   - Detailed trust score and historical efficiency profiling.
3. Simulation (Risk Lab V6.2):
   - The platform's core testing ground.
   - Run batch audits on specific city clusters (Chennai, Coimbatore, etc.).
   - Visualizes the 3-phase audit workflow:
     - Phase 1: Multi-Peril Disruption Analysis.
     - Phase 2: Integrity & Trust Verification.
     - Phase 3: Adaptive Parametric Settlement.
4. Payout Logs (Audit Ledger):
   - Immutable log of all historic settlements and mitigations.

B. Rider Mobile Portal
1. Dashboard (Resilience Hub):
   - Live "Protected" status indicator.
   - Local Zone Telemetry (Live rain, wind, and conditions).
   - Policy Overview (Tier, Weekly Premium, Coverage Floor).
2. Rider Policy:
   - Detailed breakdown of what triggers their specific plan.
   - Multipliers and coverage caps.
3. Settlement Status:
   - Personal ledger of payouts received.
   - "Integrity: Verified" status for trusted partners.
4. On-Ride Tracking:
   - Real-time monitoring component while the rider is in the field.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. API & Integration Summary
- Open-Meteo Integration: Fetches current_weather and hourly_precipitation via latitude/longitude for dynamic settlement triggers.
- Firebase Core: Manages persistent storage for Rider IDs and Payout logs.
- Framer Motion Architecture: Drives the "Premium" feel with neural mesh backgrounds and smooth layout transitions.
